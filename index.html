🏢 What is BarCloud?

BarCloud (by ASAP Systems) is a cloud-based inventory management and asset tracking platform built for organizations of all sizes — from small businesses to global enterprises. Its defining trait is combining inventory control and asset tracking in a single system, a capability most competitors split across two separate products.

🎯 Target Market

SMBs to large enterprises. Government, education, IT, healthcare, logistics, military, manufacturing

☁️ Deployment

Cloud-hosted, self-hosted, or on-premise. Flexible for any IT infrastructure requirement

📱 Mobile

iOS & Android apps. Back camera doubles as barcode scanner. Voice control feature available

💰 Pricing

From ~$45/user/month (billed annually). Lite, Standard, and Professional tiers. Custom enterprise quotes

🔗 Key Integrations

QuickBooks Online, Box, Google Workspace, Active Directory, SAML, Zapier, public API

🤖 AI Features

BarCloud AI: demand forecasting, inventory optimization, conversational interface for reports & actions

🏆 BarCloud USPs (Unique Selling Points)

🔗 True Inv. + Asset Unification

The only platform combining full inventory management AND full asset tracking under one login, one dashboard, one cost. Competitors force you to buy two systems.

♻️ Stock-to-Asset Reclassification

Instantly converts a stock item into a tracked asset while preserving its ID history — unique lifecycle continuity no competitor offers.

👤 My Work Assets Portal

Non-licensed users can view, request, and report on their own assigned assets without a paid seat — lowers total cost for large orgs.

🤖 BarCloud AI

Integrated AI with demand forecasting, ML-based optimization, and a conversational interface to run queries and reports — rare in this market segment.

🎙️ Voice Control (Mobile)

Voice-driven mobile transactions — a first in the inventory/asset tracking space, reducing friction for warehouse and field workers.

☁️ Flexible Deployment

Rare cloud + on-premise + self-hosted flexibility. Most SaaS competitors are cloud-only, making BarCloud appealing to government and regulated industries.

🔑 No Extra Hardware Needed

Any iOS 6+ or Android 4+ device becomes a scanner — removes costly hardware purchasing barriers for rapid deployment.

🏛️ Enterprise-Grade Configurability

Every workflow, report, field, and permission level is configurable — designed for complex organizations in government, military, education, and logistics.

⚠️ Gaps & Competitive Weaknesses

📡 No RFID Support

EZO and Wasp AssetCloud support RFID bulk audits. BarCloud lacks RFID, a significant gap for high-volume warehouse operations needing fast sweeps.

🌐 No GPS / Real-time Location

Competitors like Samsara offer live GPS and geofencing. BarCloud has no real-time location tracking for assets in the field.

📴 Limited Offline Mode

Requires a stable internet connection. Fishbowl and some others offer stronger offline capabilities, which matters for remote worksites.

🛒 No Recurring Reservations / Carts

EZO's "carts" and recurring reservation workflows are superior for equipment-heavy organizations (AV, construction, labs). BarCloud lacks this.

🛍️ No E-commerce Integration

No native Shopify, Amazon, or WooCommerce connectors — limits appeal for retail/e-commerce businesses vs. Zoho Inventory or Cin7.

🎫 No Helpdesk Integration

EZO and Asset Panda integrate with Zendesk and Jira for IT asset workflows. BarCloud has no native ITSM/helpdesk connectors.

💸 Pricing Transparency

Unlike competitors with fully public pricing, BarCloud's enterprise tiers require contacting sales, creating friction for self-serve buyers.

🔒 No Free Tier

AssetTiger is free for up to 250 assets; Sortly has a free plan. BarCloud has no free tier, which limits top-of-funnel adoption among SMBs and startups.
